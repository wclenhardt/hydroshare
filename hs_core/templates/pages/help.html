What is MyHPOM?

TCC is partnering with hospitals, community hospices, AARP and an SECU-supported estate planning law firm to offer a persistent and discoverable online tool for individuals’ advance directives and medical information. The online tool will facilitate the adoption of advance care planning among younger and more diverse populations and will provide the ability for individuals to link their advance care plans and health information to the people named as healthcare agent(s) and others they want to alert to their healthcare wishes. The linkages can be to family, friends, clinicians or other representatives, all chosen by the individual.

Why use MyHPOM?

Save your data online safely and share them to get feedback from other people. MyHPOM provides convenient data uploading function and save data safely online. It also has access control ability to provide user with multiple ways of sharing their data and the corresponding metadata. Besides, the system provides the function of commenting on the shared data and models, so as to help user get constructive feedback from other people.

Get credit of your data and models when cited by others. Each data or model uploaded in the MyHPOM system is assigned citation information. What’s more, in the near future the system will provide a DOI for the data or models which are approved and published in the MyHPOM system.

Easy access to hydrologic data and models shared by other users or third part organizations. MyHPOM provides powerful searching functions to help the user find the data and models they are looking for. These data and models will be from other users or third party organization such as USGS, CUAHSI HydroServer, Earth Exchange, etc.

Collaborate with other people for hydrological research. MyHPOM provides the social media functionality to enhance collaboration for hydrologic research. User will be able to create or join the groups they have interests in. And the group will provide functions for collaborations among group members to share data and models, participate in discussions, and add group blogs.

Why is MyHPOM different?

Different from existing online tools, the My Health Peace of Mind platform provides users no-cost access, allows easy updates to information and documents and provides links to hospitals nationwide who can access an individual’s information in an emergency. With our community partners, we expect to grow the number of advance care plans completed by x% while improving their discoverability.

What is a resource?

In MyHPOM, we define a resource as digital content which includes the science data files or model files and their corresponding metadata information. MyHPOM supports multiple resource types including Generic, Geographic Raster, Model Progam, Model Instance, SWAT Model Instance, Multidimensional (NetCDF), Time Series, Web App, and Geographic Feature (ESRI Shapefiles). Information on resource types and metadata terms can be found here.

How do I create a resource?

  1. Sign in to MyHPOM.
  2. Click on "My Resources" in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
  3. Click on "+Create new" at the top of the page.
  4. Select your resource type from the dropdown menu.
  5. Enter a Title.
  6. Optionally Add your data files stored on your local computer to add to your resource.
  7. Optionally Add your data files stored stored in your iRODS datagrid to your resource.
  8. Click the "Create Resource" button.
  9. A new page will appear where you can add or edit Metadata, Content, Sharing, Comments, and Add files.

How do I view, download, edit, or delete a resource?

  1. Sign in to MyHPOM.
  2. Click on "My resources" in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
  3. Click one or more of the checkboxes "Owned by me, "Editable by me," "Viewable by me," and/or "Favorites" on the top of the page.
  4. Optionally enter search criteria in the Search box.
  5. Click on resource Title to take you to that resource page.
  6. A new page will appear where you can Share, Delete, and Download a resource as well as perform various options on it.

How do I comment on or rate a resource?

  1. Sign in to MyHPOM.
  2. Click on "My resources" in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
  3. Select the Title of the resource you would like to rate or comment on.
  4. A new page will appear where you can comment on or rate a resource as well as perform various options on it. You may also rate a comment by clicking the "+1" next to the comment.
